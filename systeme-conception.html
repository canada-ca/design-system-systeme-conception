Systeme conception

From Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat

The Canada.ca design system helps Governement of Canada's institution to develop digital content. Its two main goals are to:

  • build and strenghten a strong digital brand for the Governement of Canada
  • provide user-tested patterns and templates designed to help people complete their tasks
  • reduce development costs and efforts accross institutions

For more information on Canada.ca and how it's organized, read the Canada.ca Content and Information Architecture Specification.

The Canada.ca design sytem contains:

  • Mandatory templates
  • Recommended templates
  • Common design patterns

You can also use the interactive template selector to find templates and patterns for the content you want to publish.
